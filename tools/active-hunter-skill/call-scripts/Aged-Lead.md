# Aged Internet Lead Script — Van Noy Real Estate

**Goal:** re-open the conversation, qualify timeline, book if seller-intent shows. Low pressure.

## Opener
"Hi {name}, David Van Noy with Van Noy Real Estate. You looked at homes with us a while back —
I'm cleaning up my list and didn't want to drop you if you're still in the market. Are you?"

## Discovery
- "Still looking, already bought, or plans changed?"
- "Renting or own now? If you own — any thought of selling that one?" *(turn buyers into sellers)*
- "What would have to be true for you to make a move in the next few months?"

## Value hooks
- "If you're buying, I'll set you up with a private, no-spam list that fits — and **Love It or
  Leave It** so there's no risk."
- "If you own, most people here are sitting on real equity — want a quick value on your place?"

## Objections
- *"Already working with someone."* → "No problem — are you under a written agreement, or just
  casually? If casual, worth comparing."
- *"Just browsing."* → "Cool. I'll keep you on the no-pressure list and check back. Quick — buy
  window: this year, or further out?"

## Close
If seller-intent or a near-term buy → "Let's grab 20 minutes." → **run `vnre-book-appointment`**.
Otherwise → tag the real timeline in FUB and drop into the nurture sequence.
