# FSBO Script — Van Noy Real Estate

**Goal of the call:** book a listing appointment (`LA:`). Not to "sell." To get face-to-face.

## Opener
"Hi {name}, David Van Noy with Van Noy Real Estate. I saw your home on {street} is for sale by
owner. I'm not calling to list it today — I help a lot of sellers in {city} and I had two quick
questions. Got 30 seconds?"

## Discovery (listen, don't pitch)
- "What's got you selling — and where are you headed next?"
- "If you got your price without the hassle, when would you ideally be moved?"
- "How'd you land on your asking number?"
- "What's your plan if it hasn't sold in 2–3 weeks?"

## Value hooks (use the real VNRE USPs)
- "Most FSBOs net **less** after a buyer's agent commission and negotiation — I can show you the
  actual numbers for {city}."
- "We have an **Offer on Your Home, Guaranteed** option and **commission as low as 2%** — you may
  keep more *and* skip the work."
- "**Love It or Leave It** and a **Cancellation Guarantee** — zero risk to talk."

## Objections
- *"I don't want to pay commission."* → "Fair. Let me show you net-to-net — FSBO vs. listed. If
  FSBO truly nets you more, I'll tell you to keep doing it."
- *"I'll try it myself first."* → "Smart. Give me 20 minutes so you have a pricing + marketing plan
  in your back pocket if it stalls. Costs you nothing."
- *"Just send me info."* → "I'll do one better — I'll bring a real CMA. Tuesday 5:30 or Wednesday 6?"

## Close → appointment
"Let's put 20 minutes on the calendar. I'll bring the numbers; you decide nothing on the spot.
{Day} at {time} work?" → capture details → **run `vnre-book-appointment`**.
