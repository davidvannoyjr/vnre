# Expired Listing Script — Van Noy Real Estate

**Goal:** book the `LA:`. They're motivated and frustrated — be the confident pro who has a plan.

## Opener
"Hi {name}, David Van Noy, Van Noy Real Estate. Your home on {street} came off the market — I'm
guessing you're still planning to move?"

## Discovery
- "What do you think went wrong last time?"
- "Where are you trying to go, and by when?"
- "What would your last agent need to have done differently to earn a second shot?"
- "If we could get it sold at the right price, does that change things?"

## Value hooks
- "Homes don't expire because they can't sell — they expire on **price, marketing, or both**. I fix
  both. Let me show you my **plan of action** and the **media reach** behind every VNRE listing."
- "**#1 listing presence in Johnson and Jackson County** — exposure your last listing didn't get."
- "**Cancellation Guarantee** — if I don't perform, you fire me. No hostage situations."

## Objections
- *"Already re-listing with someone."* → "Then a second opinion costs you nothing and might save the
  deal. 20 minutes?"
- *"Taking a break from the market."* → "Understood. Let's at least nail the price + plan so when
  you're ready you launch strong, not stale."
- *"What's different about you?"* → "I'll show you — comps, marketing, timeline in days not 'soon.'
  Tuesday or Wednesday evening?"

## Close → appointment
"Give me 20 minutes to show you exactly why it didn't sell and how I'd fix it. {Day} {time}?"
→ capture details → **run `vnre-book-appointment`**.
