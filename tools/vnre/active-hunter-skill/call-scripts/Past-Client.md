# Past Client Script — Van Noy Real Estate

**Goal:** the cheapest GCI you'll ever earn — repeat + referral. Lead with care, not the ask.

## Opener
"Hey {name}, David Van Noy — no agenda, just thinking about you. How's the {street} house treating
you? … Family good?"

## Bridge to value
- *(equity)* "Heads up — homes like yours in {city} are up roughly {gain} since you bought. Want me
  to send a quick value update? No pressure."
- *(anniversary)* "Can you believe it's been {N} years? Crazy how time flies."
- *(refi, if rate high)* "If you're still at that higher rate, my lender partner can run a refi
  number — could be real monthly savings. Want the intro?"

## The referral ask (always, gently)
"One favor — who's the next person you know making a move? I'll take the same care of them I took of
you. You can just text me their name."

## Objections
- *"Not moving / nobody comes to mind."* → "All good. If someone pops up, I'm a text away. And I'll
  keep that value update coming so you always know where you stand."

## Close
Move/refi signal → **run `vnre-book-appointment`** or route the refi to the lender partner.
Otherwise → log to FUB; the **Database & COI engine** keeps the cadence (equity / anniversary / referral).
