# Circle / COI / Geo-farm Script — Van Noy Real Estate

**Goal:** stay top-of-mind, surface moves, earn referrals. Relationship first, ask second.

## COI / Sphere opener
"Hey {name}, David — not a sales call, just checking in. How's everything? … Quick thing while I've
got you: do you know anyone thinking about buying or selling in the next few months? I've got
capacity and I'll take great care of them."

## Geo-farm / Just-Sold-Just-Listed opener
"Hi {name}, David Van Noy with Van Noy Real Estate. I just {sold/listed} a home on {nearby street}
in {city} — wanted neighbors to know what it went for. Curious what your place might be worth these
days?"

## Value hooks
- "We're the **#1 listing presence** in the neighborhood — when you're ready, your home gets the
  most eyes."
- "Happy to run a no-obligation value update — a lot of folks here have more equity than they think."

## Objections
- *"Not moving."* → "Totally fine — I'll keep you posted on the neighborhood. And if anyone you know
  is moving, send 'em my way."
- *"Just tell me what it's worth."* → "Easy — I'll text a quick range, and if you want the precise
  number I'll swing by for 15 minutes."

## Close
Move signal → **run `vnre-book-appointment`**. Otherwise → confirm preferred channel, log to FUB,
and let the **Database & COI engine** carry the ongoing touches.
