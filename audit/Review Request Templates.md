# Review Request Templates — feed your star ratings into AI

> Reviews drive two things AI shows: your star rating (AggregateRating in answers/rich
> results) and the volume signal that says "this is a real, trusted business." Goal: a
> steady stream of reviews on **Google** (highest AI weight) and **Zillow** (real estate
> authority). Voice: DVN — direct, confident, no begging.

## The rule
- Ask **every** closing. Closing day or 1–2 days after, while it's high.
- Send **one direct link** — friction kills review rates. Pre-build:
  - Google review link: from your Google Business Profile → "Ask for reviews" → copy the short link.
  - Zillow review link: zillow.com/profile/VanNoyRE → "Write a review" URL.
- Default ask = **Google first** (highest AI weight). Send Zillow as the follow-up to your best clients.
- Personalize the first line. Never mass-blast identically.

> [First name] — congrats again on [closing/the new place]. It was a pleasure getting this done for you. If you've got 60 seconds, a quick Google review means a lot and helps other [city] sellers find me: [Google link]. Thank you. — David

> [First name] — one more ask: a quick review on Zillow helps buyers and sellers know what to expect working with me. Here's the link: [Zillow link]. Appreciate you.

## 3. In-person ask (script for closing day)
> "One thing that really helps me — would you leave a quick Google review about your experience? I'll text you the link right now so it's easy." [Send the SMS template on the spot.]

---

## 4. Handling a great verbal compliment
When a client says something glowing in person or by text:
> "That means a lot — would you mind putting that exact thing in a Google review? Here's the link." [Send link immediately.]
*The best reviews are the ones you capture in the moment they're said.*

---

## 5. What to do with reviews once you have them
- **Volume target:** aim for a steady drip — even 2–3 new Google reviews/month compounds fast.
- **Reply to every review** (Google + Zillow) — a short, professional reply is a freshness + engagement signal AI and Google reward.
- **Feed ratings into schema:** once counts are stable, update `Schema-JSON-LD-Block.md` AggregateRating (ratingValue + reviewCount) so your stars can appear in AI answers and rich results. Log it in `Audit Log.md` (task #26).
- **Never** incentivize or fake reviews — Google/Zillow penalize it and it poisons your entity trust.
