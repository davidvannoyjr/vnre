The Principle

AI is the multiplier on your disciplines — not a replacement for them. It won't prospect for you, build your database, or close the deal. It makes you prospect more, follow up faster, present sharper, and serve deeper with the same hours in the day. Master the fundamentals first. Then bolt AI onto each one.

// THE DECISION FILTER

Before You Adopt Any Tool

Every AI tool — like every other business decision — runs through the same four questions. If it fails any of them, skip it. This keeps you out of shiny-object purgatory.

01

Does it drive GCI?

If it doesn't move you toward listings, conversions, or commission, it's a toy. Pass.

02

Pays off in 90 days?

Real return inside a quarter — not "someday." No pilots that never ship.

03

Simple enough daily?

If you won't use it every day, it's friction. Simple beats clever, every time.

// THE FIVE BUILDING BLOCKS

Where AI Gives an Agent Leverage

These are the five places AI pays off fastest for a real estate agent. Each has a 30-day starter project and a copy-paste prompt to run today. Start with your weakest.

📞
Building Block 01

Prospecting Prep

AI gives you sharper, more confident calls. Before you dial, it pulls neighborhood angles, recent market stats, and talking points so you never open cold. It doesn't make the call — it makes you better on it.

Your First 30-Day Project

Before every prospecting block, spend 5 minutes getting Claude to brief you on the area and the market. Track whether your conversations get longer.

Copy this starter prompt
You're my prospecting coach. I'm about to call homeowners in [NEIGHBORHOOD], [CITY/STATE]. Give me: 3 conversation openers that aren't salesy, 3 current talking points about this local market a homeowner would care about, and 2 smart questions that get them talking about their plans. Keep it tight and natural — I'm reading this live on a call.

Building Block 02

Database & Follow-Up

Your database is the business. AI helps you write follow-ups fast, segment by motivation, and re-engage the contacts you've gone quiet on. The fortune is in the follow-up — AI removes the excuse of "I didn't have time to write it."

Your First 30-Day Project

Pull your 25 most-neglected past clients. Have Claude draft a personal, no-pressure re-engagement message for each. Send 5 a day.

Copy this starter prompt
Write a short, warm re-engagement text to a past client I haven't spoken to in over a year. Their name is [NAME], I helped them buy/sell [PROPERTY/YEAR]. No hard sell — I just want to reconnect and stay top of mind. Sound like a real person, not a marketer. Give me 2 versions.

Building Block 03

CMA & Pricing Story

AI won't pull live MLS data — but feed it your comps and it will structure a clean pricing narrative, explain the numbers in plain English, and prep you to defend the price at the table. Faster prep, sharper presentation.

Your First 30-Day Project

On your next listing appointment, paste your comps into Claude and have it draft the pricing story you'll walk the seller through. Practice it out loud.

Copy this starter prompt
Here are recent comparable sales I pulled: [PASTE COMPS — address, price, sqft, beds/baths, sold date]. My seller's home is [DETAILS]. Help me build a clear pricing story I can present: where this home sits vs. the comps, the recommended list price range, and how I'd explain it to a seller who thinks their home is worth more. Plain language.

Building Block 04

Skill-Building & Role-Play

AI is a role-play partner that never gets tired and never judges. Drill your weakest scripts and objections every morning. Repetition builds mastery — and now you can rep against a live partner before you ever dial a real lead.

Your First 30-Day Project

Pick the objection you fumble most. Run 5 minutes of AI role-play on it every morning for two weeks. Watch your real-world response tighten.

Copy this starter prompt
Role-play with me. You're a FSBO seller who's skeptical of agents and says "I'm not paying a commission." Stay in character and push back realistically. After each of my responses, break character for one line to coach me on what worked and what to sharpen. Start by giving me the objection.

Building Block 05

Marketing & Content

Automate the production, never the relationship. AI writes listing descriptions, social captions, and Just-Listed / Just-Sold copy at scale — so you spend your time face-to-face, not staring at a blank caption box.

Your First 30-Day Project

For your next listing, generate the MLS description, 3 social captions, and an email blurb in one sitting. Bank a content library you reuse.

Copy this starter prompt
Write listing marketing for this home: [ADDRESS, BEDS/BATHS, SQFT, KEY FEATURES, NEIGHBORHOOD]. Give me: 1 polished MLS description (~150 words), 3 short social captions with a different hook each, and a 2-line "Just Listed" email teaser. Confident and professional — no clichés like "must see" or "won't last."

// THE GUARDRAILS

What AI Never Touches

The tools exist to protect the relationship — never to replace it. Two lines you don't cross.

AI augments. You close.

  • The relationship, the trust, the close — always you. AI preps, you perform.
  • Never automate the human moment: the live conversation, the hard call, the negotiation.
  • Always read what AI gives you before it goes out. You own every word with your name on it.
  • AI handles the repetitive and the analytical. You keep the relationship and the judgment.

Protect your data.

  • Draw a clear line on what client data never goes into a public AI model.
  • No full SSNs, financials, or signed contracts pasted into a chat box.
  • Use first names and general details for drafting — strip the sensitive specifics.
  • When in doubt, leave it out. The follow-up isn't worth a privacy breach.
