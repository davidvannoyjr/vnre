# VNRE Market Updates

Monthly KC market update content package for the VNRE database and social channels.

## Cadence
Monthly, after MLS month-end data is available.

## Inputs (drop into this folder)
- Heartland MLS trend report PDFs: YTD + last-year, per county (Johnson KS, Jackson MO)
- Days-on-market / median price exports per county
- Any matrix/history sales exports

## Outputs
- `<Month Year> KC Market Update - Full Content Package.md` — long-form update, video script, social cuts
- `<Month Year> Market Update - FUB Email Template.html` — paste-ready Follow Up Boss email

## Process
1. Read the new PDFs, pull MoM/YoY: median price, DOM, closed sales, inventory — per county.
2. Write the content package in DVN voice (`dvn-voice` skill). Data-first, no hype.
3. Build the FUB HTML email from the package.
4. Keep prior months' files here as history.
