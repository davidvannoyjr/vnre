# Project: White-Label Broker Model (VNRE-as-platform)

**Purpose (VNRE):** Evaluate running Van Noy Real Estate as a back-end / white-label broker of record — teams and agents branded as their own companies (the "Side" model), VNRE takes a split/fee for tech, compliance, coaching, marketing, branding, office, and lead-gen.

**Cadence:** On-demand research/strategy. Not recurring.

**Inputs:** MO + KS license law (RSMo 339, 20 CSR 2250-8.070; K.S.A. 58-3086, K.A.R. 86-3-7), NAR Code of Ethics Art. 12, Heartland MLS rules, competitor models (Side, eXp, Real, Pinnacle, Fathom, PLACE).

**Outputs:**
- `2026-06 White-Label Broker Model - Legal Research.md` — full cited research + build plan + decision filter.

**Key findings (do not relitigate):**
- A fully invisible brokerage is illegal in MO and KS. The broker name must appear on every ad; broker name + phone whenever agent contact info appears.
- Missouri has NO prominence/font-size rule — brokerage can be minimized to compliant fine print ("Brokered by VNRE"). This is the opening.
- Kansas caps the team/agent name at ≤2× the VNRE name and requires adjacency online. Design KS-compliant first; it satisfies MO automatically.
- Structure each team as a broker-owned DBA (fictitious name registered to VNRE with MO SOS, filed with MREC within 10 days) — the Side architecture.
- Closest direct competitors (Side, Pinnacle) do NOT operate in KS/MO = whitespace. DVN Coaching is the differentiator no platform bundles.

**Open verification step:** Pull verbatim MO/KS rule text from the SOS/revisor PDFs (WebFetch was blocked during research) and have a MO/KS real estate attorney review DBA-ownership + brand-portability terms before publishing any compliance template.

**Status:** Research complete 2026-06. Decision pending. Stays in `01-projects/` until DVN decides go/no-go.
