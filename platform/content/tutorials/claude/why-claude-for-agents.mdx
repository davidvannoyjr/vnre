ChatGPT and Claude are not the same tool. Using the wrong one is why some tasks feel like a fight.

## Reach for Claude when

- **You're working a long document.** Inspection reports, CMAs, contracts, disclosures. Claude handles big documents without losing the thread.
- **You want a repeatable process, not a chat.** Claude's *Projects* and *Skills* let you save a workflow and run it the same way every time.
- **Accuracy on your data matters.** Summarizing a 38-page inspection, comparing two offers, pulling the dates out of a contract.

## Reach for ChatGPT when

- You want fast, conversational drafts — social posts, quick emails.
- You're using image generation or the broad plugin ecosystem.

It's not loyalty to a brand. It's the right wrench for the bolt.

## The 10-minute test

Take your next inspection report or seller disclosure. Drop the PDF into Claude and ask:

> Summarize this for the seller in plain language. Give me the three issues that actually affect the sale and the three that are cosmetic. Don't editorialize.

That's the kind of work that used to eat your evening. Now it's a coffee break.
