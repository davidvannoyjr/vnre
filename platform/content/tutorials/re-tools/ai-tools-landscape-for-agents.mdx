There are hundreds of "AI for real estate" tools. Most are a thin wrapper on ChatGPT with a markup. Here's how to think about the categories that actually move a listing business.

## Categories worth your attention

**1. Content & listing AI** — listing descriptions, photo enhancement, social. *Verdict:* you can do most of this yourself with ChatGPT for $20. Pay only if the tool saves real time on something specific (e.g., bulk social scheduling).

**2. AI dialers** — power dialers with AI assist for list scrubbing and call notes. *Verdict:* worth it if you prospect daily. The leverage is in reps, not the AI sparkle.

**3. AI voice agents** — answer and route inbound calls 24/7. *Verdict:* high value for a solo or small team. A captured call is a lead that didn't go to the next agent. (Advanced track walks the full setup.)

**4. CMA / pricing AI** — automated comp pulls and pricing ranges. *Verdict:* useful for a first pass. Never let it set your price — it can't read a kitchen.

## The buying filter

Run every tool through this:

1. Does it drive listings, GCI, or save hours I'd otherwise pay for?
2. ROI-positive within 90 days?
3. Simple enough to use daily?
4. Does it remove friction or add another login I'll abandon?

If it fails any of these, skip it. A tool you don't open is a subscription, not leverage.
